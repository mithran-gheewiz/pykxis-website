DMAIC Methodology

Every engagement begins with a structured, data-driven improvement methodology based on Lean Six Sigma principles.

  • Define: Clarify the business objectives, customer requirements, and manufacturing challenges.
  • Measure: Collect and validate process, quality, equipment, and operational data.
  • Analyze: Apply statistical methods, process capability analysis, and machine learning to identify root causes and improvement opportunities.
  • Improve: Implement targeted process improvements, predictive models, and AI-driven solutions to enhance manufacturing performance.
  • Control: Sustain improvements through dashboards, KPI monitoring, Statistical Process Control (SPC), and continuous process optimization.

Process Capability Analysis: Cpk & Ppk

As part of our Measure and Analyze phases, Pykxis Consulting evaluates process capability using Cpk and Ppk analysis to support fact-based decision-making and continuous improvement.

Why Process Capability Matters

Process capability analysis provides statistical evidence to determine whether a process can consistently meet customer or design specifications.

Cpk and Ppk evaluations help identify process centering, variation, and performance gaps so teams can take informed corrective action.

Control Limits vs. Specification Limits

Control limits represent the voice of the process and show how the process behaves over time.

Specification limits represent the voice of the customer and define the requirements the process must satisfy.
